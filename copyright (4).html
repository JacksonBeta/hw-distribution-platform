Copyright Registration Process

  • Complete Our Form

    Fill out the copyright registration form with your film details and contact information.

  • Receive Your Registration Code

    We'll generate a unique Hollywood Weekly copyright registration code for your film.

  • Visit the US Copyright Office

    Go to copyright.gov and begin the registration process for your film.

  • Include Your Registration Code

    When submitting your application, include your Hollywood Weekly registration code in the "Additional Information" section.

  • Complete Copyright Registration

    Finish the US Copyright Office application process and pay the required fee to secure your copyright.
