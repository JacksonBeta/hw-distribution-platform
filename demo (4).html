Platform Overview

The Hollywood Weekly Distribution Platform connects independent filmmakers with major streaming services including Google TV, Prime Video, Peacock, and Apple TV. This platform allows filmmakers to upload their content once and distribute it across multiple platforms, while tracking performance and revenue in real-time.

Multi-Platform Distribution
Distribute your films to Google TV, Prime Video, Peacock, and Apple TV from a single platform.
Performance Analytics
Track views, engagement, and revenue across all platforms in real-time.
Revenue Tracking
Monitor earnings from multiple revenue models including subscription, pay-per-view, and ad-supported.
Monthly Statements
Receive detailed monthly statements with revenue breakdowns by platform and film.
